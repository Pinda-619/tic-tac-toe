## How to Submit an Open Application

### Step 1 — Find the right contact
- Go to the company's **career page** and look for a general application or contact form
- Search on **LinkedIn** for: `HR Manager [Company]` or `Talent Acquisition [Company]` or the relevant department head (e.g. `Supply Chain Manager`)
- Aim for someone in HR or the specific department, not a generic inbox

### Step 2 — Write a short, targeted email
Keep it under 200 words. Structure:
1. **Who you are** — study, year, focus
2. **Why this company** — be specific, show you did research
3. **What you offer** — relevant skills or coursework
4. **The ask** — internship from August/September 2026, attach CV

### Step 3 — Follow up
- Wait 1–2 weeks, then send one polite follow-up if no reply
- Connect with the contact on LinkedIn after sending the email

### Step 4 — Track everything
- Log every application in the tracker (see `application_tracker.md`)
